📅 Each Day

How a Day Works

1
Train or Audition
Use your energy on training activities to build skills, or visit the Job Board to apply for roles. You can do multiple activities in a day as long as you have energy.
2
Visit Your Film Set
If you're currently filming, go to set and choose your effort level. This is the only way to push film quality above 60.
3
End the Day
Hit Rest to advance to the next day. This processes audition results, advances your films, and fully restores your energy to 100.
4
Handle What Comes
Check your Inbox for offers. Respond to any controversies on your Dashboard. Accept or decline any private Invitations.
